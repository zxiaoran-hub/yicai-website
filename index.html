关于我们About Us

中国化妆品供应链服务平台 Supply Chain Platform for China's Cosmetics Industry

异采（YiCai）做的事情说起来简单：把中国化妆品产业链上的资源串起来。从备案咨询、原料和包材采购，到代加工生产和品牌代运营，我们帮客户省掉到处找供应商、反复踩坑的麻烦，把产品顺利做出来、卖出去。 YiCai connects the dots across China's cosmetics supply chain. From regulatory filing, raw material and packaging sourcing, to contract manufacturing and brand operation — we help clients skip the hassle of finding suppliers and navigating regulations, and get products made and sold smoothly.
